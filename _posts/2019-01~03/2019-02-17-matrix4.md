## AB 逆矩阵

逆矩阵



$$
AA^{-1} = I = A^{-1}A \\
(AB)(B^{-1}A^{-1}) = A(BB^{-1})A^{-1} = AIA^{-1} = I
$$



转置的逆
$$
(AA^{-1})^T = (A^{-1})^T A^T = I^T = I \\
A^T(A^T)^{-1} = \\
Row1Col1^T = (ARow 1 \cdot BCol1)^T = (BCol1)^T \cdot (ARow1)^T \\
\text 行列式相乘后的转置等于矩阵转置并调换顺序
$$
